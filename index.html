About

Credibility, earned in production.

We've been building production-grade software since 2020 — quietly shipping things that matter.

Left Coast Stack LLC is a software development company in Spokane, Washington, founded in 2020. We partner with organizations that need custom engineering—dashboards, platforms, marketing sites, and AI-enabled features—delivered with an emphasis on modern front-end stacks, full-stack delivery, and reliable production systems.

We work in the ecosystems teams already use: React, Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind and component libraries, Vue.js where it fits, Python with Django and Flask, Node.js and Express, React Native and Expo, major cloud providers, and mature DevOps and testing practice. Engagements are built around close collaboration, clear handoff, and software that holds up to real traffic, security expectations, and ongoing operations—not demo-day polish.

Our clients are product teams, enterprises, and startups with serious engineering needs: internal tools, customer-facing products, CMS and content migrations, performance and SEO work, AI-enabled features, and real-time systems. If you need a partner who can ship and document the result, we're easy to talk to.
