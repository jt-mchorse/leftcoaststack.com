About

Credibility, earned in production.

We've been building production-grade software since 2020 — quietly shipping things that matter.

Left Coast Stack LLC is a software development company in Spokane, Washington, founded in 2020. We partner with organizations that need custom engineering—dashboards, platforms, marketing sites, and AI-enabled features—delivered with an emphasis on modern front-end stacks, full-stack delivery, and reliable production systems.

We work in the ecosystems teams already use: React, Next.js, TypeScript, Python, Django, React Native, cloud providers, and mature DevOps practice. Engagements are built around close collaboration, clear handoff, and software that holds up to real traffic, security expectations, and ongoing operations—not demo-day polish.

Our clients are product teams, enterprises, and startups with serious engineering needs: internal tools, customer-facing products, migrations, performance work, and real-time systems. If you need a partner who can ship and document the result, we're easy to talk to.
